# Crane (crane)

Crane Co. (NYSE: CR) and Crane NXT (NYSE: CXT) are industrial manufacturing companies that resulted from the 2023 split of the legacy Crane Holdings, Co. Crane Co. is a diversified manufacturer of highly engineered industrial products serving aerospace and electronics, process flow technologies, and engineered materials markets. Crane NXT is a global leader in trusted technology solutions for payment, security, and authentication including currency, banknote, and authentication products.
